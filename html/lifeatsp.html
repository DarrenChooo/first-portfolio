In PFP

PFP is a programme for the top Normal (Academic) students. Students enrolled into PFP will skip the fifth year of secondary school and be admitted into the polytechnic.

In my PFP year, it was exciting as we have a range of modules for us, such as science, programming and effective communication modules.

There was a project where we gather in groups of 5 to make a prototype on aiding people on how to play chess.

My Poly Life

Why SOC

The world today is fast changing at this current state due to the advancements of technology. Singapore's economy will slowly transit with the new digital innovations. Our nation will expect to grow into a field of technology, data analytics and artificial intelligence, cybersecurity and app developments.

With your appearance in SOC, it opens up many pathways of jobs for your future. The school will assist you in learning and discovering as many new skills as possible, ensuring that you will set in the right attitude and the best capabilities you have for your lifelong journey.
