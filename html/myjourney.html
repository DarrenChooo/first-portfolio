Who was I in Secondary School

Before coming into Singapore Polytechnic (SP), I was a student at Dunman Secondary School. My CCA in secondary school was the Multimedia club. The multimedia club teaches us how to edit photos/videos that we shot in school. I joined this club as I was curious about the digital single-lens reflex (DSLR) camera.

My journey towards SP

Honestly, I was not an outstanding student for my academics in secondary school. I was a Normal Academic student and took the Polytechnic Foundation Programme (PFP) route in SP before my diploma. I did badly for my Mid-year examination, which was an EMB3 of 27 points. Afterwards, I put in more effort for my N(A)-Level examination and secured a spot in SP. Therefore, we should not give up as it does not matter how slowly we go, as long as we do not stop, we will reach our final goal.
