My Course

The course that I am in now is DCITP. DCITP is a course that helps students that are unsure to make an informed choice on which course to join after a semester. This course helps broaden your knowledge about Infocomm Technology (IT) and get a clearer view of occupations in the individual Infocomm sectors.


For more information about Diploma in Common Infocomm Technology Programme click here.


Furthermore, this course helps to build a strong foundation in programming and IT fundamentals. At the end of semester 1, you get to choose from 3 courses on which sector you wish to pursue.


The 3 courses are

  1. Diploma in Applied AI and Analytics - Click here to find out more!
  2. Diploma in Infocomm Security Management - Click here to find out more!
  3. Diploma in information Technology - Click here to find out more!

My Future Career

After Semester 1, I would like to pursue the Diploma of Infocomm Security Management (DISM) course. I have always dreamt of working in the field of being a cybersecurity analyst.


Cybersecurity analysts plan and carry out security measures to protect a company's computer network and systems. Also, they monitor their company's network for any breaches in security.


Demand for cybersecurity professionals has grown by 6.6 times over the last three years. Moreover, cybersecurity is needed virtually in every industry, such as retail, transport and government services.


This means that I can work with a variety of portfolios and immerse myself in these different industries. In addition, soft skills like communication, critical and creative thinking are highly coveted by cybersecurity firms.
